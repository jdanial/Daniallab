Visual identity
We believe that a strong visual identity underlies reproducible, ambitious, and curiosity-driven research.
[↑] Reduced logo mark
Identity
Our mark represents the diffraction pattern seen when observing single molecules under a light microscope, reflecting our research focus on single-molecule imaging. The logo represents the letter 'D,' creating an authoritative mark that is unique to the Danial Lab.
[↑] Full logo mark
Typography
The pictorial mark is paired with a text mark enclosed in an outlined square canvas that aligns with the logo's construction. The text, along with all related branding materials, utilises the Lexend Deca - an open-source developed by Dr. Bonnie Shaver-Troup in collaboration with Google to improve reading fluency.
[↑] Reduced colour palette
Colours
We use fluorescent green as a brand colour that represents our research focus on fluorescence microscopy. We pair this with a wide range of fluorescent (neon) colours and grey scales that are broadly useable across presentations, posters, plots, figures, and documents.
